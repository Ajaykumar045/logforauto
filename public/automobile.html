The Koenigsegg Jesko has a 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that produces 1280 horsepower (hp) on standard gasoline and 1600 hp on E85 biofuel. Koenigsegg developed its own engines from scratch, including the Jesko's engine, control systems, and transmissions. The Jesko is a Swedish hypercar named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the father of the brand's founder and CEO.
